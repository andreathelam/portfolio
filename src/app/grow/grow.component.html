OVERVIEW

"How much water should I give her? Is the room humidity ok?" As aspiring plant owners, Lisa and I realized the difficulties of plant care, but we found that the responsibility of nuturing another living being contributed to our self-improvement and self-growth.

So...we envisioned a plant delivery service with a mobile application that helps people manage and organize their plant family, along with providing the necessary information needed to care for each plant.

Grow is subscription-based service that sends customers a new plant on a set basis and provides the resources to support the optimal well-being of their plants. The goal is to cultivate feelings of empowerment through the nurturing of plants.

Understanding the User

When conducting my research, I wanted to understand the motivation behind people who are passionate about plant care and to learn how people, who own multiple plants, take care of their green family.

Method: Contextual Inquiry

Key Research Question

What challenges do people who own a lot of plants have when organizing and tracking the care for each plant?

To design the mobile tool, I observed a participant in their daily morning plant care routine. The participant had 16 plants that were accumulated over the past year, and she had intermediate knowledge in taking care of plants.


What I learned from the Contextual Inquiry

The participant used the Notes app on her iPhone to track and manage all of her plants. She had found the plant care information from various online resources and recorded it down everytime she added a new plant to her collection. The information included:

  • how often the plant should be watered and the last date it was watered
  • the amount of sunlight it should have
  • the type of environment and humidity it likes
  • any additional notes such as best seasons for fertilization

The participant described her process as “tedious and time-consuming” and wanted a more quick method in attaining the care instructions for her plants and organizing her plants based on categories.

Persona

With what I learned from my research, I created a persona of my target user. I used this to focus the app on satisfying the user’s needs.

Wireframes

The Pages

Home

Discover our variety of plants and filter based on categories and levels. Clicking on each plant will lead to its plant page with further description and care instructions.

Favorites

Showcases the user’s adopted plants. Each card displays a picture of the plant, along with its name. Clicking on each adopted plant will lead to its health page, where the user can track their care for their plant by date and time.

Family

Informs the user of the plant being delivered next. Also shows a list of the other plants the user has favorited.

Profile

Displays the user’s name, picture, number of adopted plants, and achievements. Also where the user can access their settings.
